Once upon a time, in a quiet little town, there lived a small gray cat named Milo. Milo was not like the other cats — while they spent their days napping in the sun, he was endlessly curious about the world beyond the garden fence. Every morning, he would sit by the gate, watching birds fly freely across the sky, wondering what adventures waited just beyond his home.

One day, gathering all his courage, Milo slipped through a tiny gap in the fence and set off on his very first adventure. He discovered rustling leaves, friendly strangers, and even a sparkling stream where he saw his reflection shimmer. Quick FAQ

native details/summary
Why does my cat knock things off tables?
Curiosity + play + attention. Add more enrichment (toys, play sessions, climbing spots) and keep fragile items away from edges.
Why does my cat purr a lot?
Many cats purr when relaxed and happy, but they can also purr to self-soothe when stressed. Context matters.
How can I help my cat feel safe?
Keep a calm routine, provide hiding spots, and let your cat approach first. Trust builds steadily with gentle consistency.
