Quick FAQ

Why does my cat knock things off tables?

Sometimes it’s curiosity, sometimes play, sometimes attention.
Add more enrichment (toys, play time) and keep tempting items away.

Why does my cat purr a lot?

Many cats purr when they’re relaxed and happy.
They can also purr to self-soothe when stressed—context matters.

How can I help my cat feel safe?

Provide a calm routine, safe hiding spots, and gentle interactions.
Let your cat come to you—trust grows over time.
